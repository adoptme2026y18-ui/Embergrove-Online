# Embergrove Online - World Lore

## The World of Embergrove

Embergrove is a land shaped by ancient magical forces known as Emberhearts. These powerful sources of energy are scattered throughout the world and have influenced nature, wildlife, and civilization for centuries.

## History

Long ago, the Emberhearts brought life and prosperity to the land. Over time, kingdoms rose and fell around these magical sources.

Many secrets from the ancient age remain hidden in ruins waiting to be discovered.
