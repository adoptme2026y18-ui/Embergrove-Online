# Embergrove Online - Skills

## Overview

Skills represent a player's experience and proficiency in various activities throughout Embergrove.

Players improve skills by performing related actions.

## Gathering Skills

### Mining
Obtain ores, gems, and minerals.

### Woodcutting
Harvest wood from trees.

### Fishing
Catch fish and aquatic resources.

### Foraging
Gather plants, berries, mushrooms, and herbs.

## Crafting Skills

### Blacksmithing
Create weapons, tools, and armor.

### Carpentry
Craft furniture and wooden structures.

### Tailoring
Create clothing and fabric items.

### Cooking
Prepare food and meals.

### Alchemy
Brew potions and magical consumables.

## Farming Skills

### Farming
Plant, grow, and harvest crops.

### Animal Care
Raise and care for livestock and pets.

## Combat Skills

### Melee
Improve effectiveness with close-range weapons.

### Archery
Improve ranged weapon proficiency.

### Magic
Improve magical abilities.

## Progression

Skills gain experience through use.

Higher skill levels unlock:
- New recipes
- Better equipment
- Advanced resources
- Special abilities

## Future Skills

Additional skills may be added during development.
