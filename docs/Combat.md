# Embergrove Online - Combat

## Overview

Combat allows players to defend themselves, defeat hostile creatures, complete quests, and explore dangerous areas.

Combat should be easy to learn but rewarding to master.

## Combat Types

### Melee
Close-range combat using weapons such as:
- Swords
- Axes
- Hammers
- Spears

### Ranged
Long-range combat using:
- Bows
- Crossbows

### Magic
Combat using magical abilities such as:
- Elemental spells
- Healing spells
- Support spells

## Enemy Types

### Wildlife
Animals that may become hostile.

### Creatures
Magical beings found throughout the world.

### Corrupted Enemies
Creatures affected by dark magic.

### Dungeon Bosses
Powerful enemies guarding rare rewards.

## Health

Players have a Health value.

When Health reaches zero:
- The player is defeated.
- The player respawns at a safe location.
- Some penalties may apply.

## Equipment

Equipment affects combat performance.

### Weapons
Increase damage and unlock combat styles.

### Armor
Provides protection and bonuses.

## Party Combat

Players can group together to:
- Explore
- Complete dungeons
- Defeat bosses
- Help each other
